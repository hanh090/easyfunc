Bold CJK (Chinese-Japanese-Korean) Fonts are Difficult to Read.

Symptoms

CJK (Chinese-Japanese-Korean) fonts, when displayed in bold, are difficult to read.

Cause

CJK fonts, when displayed in bold, are sometimes difficult to read at small sizes of about 16 points or less.

Resolution

Specify the font as 'plain'.

Related Information

None.
